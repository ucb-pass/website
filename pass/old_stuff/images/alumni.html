Our Staff

Every year, PASS selects a group of individuals to represent the organization as staff members. Fifteen positions are placed among three components - Internal Affairs, Recruitment, and Retention.

components

Internal Affairs

The Internal Affairs component serves as the 'back-bone' of our organization. It currently holds 5 different positions, all with the responsibility of keeping PASS running smoothly and efficiently.

Recruitment

The recruitment component of PASS seeks to promote awareness of access to higher education. PASS aims to spread knowledge and share experiences by encouraging our future leaders to pursue higher education.

Retention

PASS serves as a resource to the Pilipino community in Berkeley by providing academic resources and social services. Working closely with the Office of Undergraduate Admissions, LEAD Center, Cesar Chavez Student Learning Center and the bridges Multicultural Resource Center, we strive to build a community of academically, politically and socially retained individuals.
