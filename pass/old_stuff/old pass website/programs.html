Recurring Programming & Events

General MeetingsMeetings open to the public occur 2-4 times per semester, with the final meeting being facilitated by our interns
MixersSocial events where new first year and transfer students can meet the upperclassmen
Study Halls/JamsHeld every Sunday to provide a room for folks to study or acquire resource materials. Study jams are held at 89 Dwinelle from 9am-9pm. Also, during dead week, the multicultural community center (mcc) is transformed into a study space from 11AM until 12AM.
Biyuti Meetingsbiyuti meetings are generally held twice a month on tuesdays
PASOCDuring the Fall, PASOC is a weekly, 2 unit course P/NP (AAADS 98). During the Spring, PASOC takes the form of multiple workshops catering to Spring admits.
RetreatsMultiple retreats are held for the various programs put on by PASS, some of these include a retreat for General Members held in the Spring semester as well as a Gender and Sexuality Awareness component retreat
