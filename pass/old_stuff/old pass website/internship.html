Intern for PASS

The PASS internship is conducted simultaneously with the Asian American 97/197 field studies course: Pilipino Issues in Education. All intern requirements are mandatory for those taking the internship for units. Non-unit takers are highly encouraged to complete the requirements in order to get the most out of this experience. Please be fully aware of the commitments that this internship entails before you apply. Although we always welcome as many interns as possible, we want to know that the interns we commit to mentoring are also just as committed to us.

Objectives and intern requirements can be found on the syllabus.

Contact

If you are interested in interning, please fill out the application.

For more information, contact Katrina-Grace Dollaga, Assistant Director of Internal Affairs (2012-2013) at adia.pass@gmail.com.
