What goes on in the Spring?

Spring may be the more challenging out of the two semesters in the sense that much can and does happen in the Spring semester such as ASUC elections, Pilipino Cultural Night, as well one of our most crucial and oldest programs known as Senior Weekend which PASS holds a strong history with. Senior Weekend, however, is not the only crucial event that PASS works towards developing, planning, and putting on from year to year, but also assisting with keeping its general programs alive and well.
