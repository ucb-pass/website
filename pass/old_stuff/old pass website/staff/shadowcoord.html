Internal Affairs

The Internal Affairs component serves as the 'back-bone' of our organization. It currently holds 5 different positions, all with the responsibility of keeping PASS running smoothly and efficiently.

Recruitment

The recruitment component of PASS seeks to promote awareness of access to higher education. PASS aims to spread knowledge and share experiences by encouraging our future leaders to pursue higher education.

Retention

PASS serves as a resource to the Pilipino campus population by providing academic resources and social services. Working closely with the Cesar Chavez Learning Center, we strive to build a community of academically, politically and socially retained individuals.

Tell Us About Your Position

As Shadow Coordinator, I am responsible for the following programming:

myPASS SHADOW - We bring small groups of students (usually less than 10), to spend the day or have an overnight stay at our campus. The goal is to create a very personal and intimate experience for visiting students.

Pre-Professional SHADOW Day, LGBT SHADOW Day - Specialized SHADOW programs, in which we bring groups of students, usually in groups of 10 - 30 per event. The Pre-Professional SHADOW is in collaboration with the PIlipino Association of Health Careers (PAHC), the Partnership for Pre-Professional Pilipinos (P4) and the Pilipino Association of Scientists, Architects, and Engineers (PASAE). This event will be held in tandem with PAHC Medical Mission Benefit Concert (MMBC).

FED SHADOW Weekend - The highlight of the year for SHADOW, we will bring about 60 students to spend with weekend at Cal and attend the Filipino Empowerment Day Conference (FED). We hope to empower students in their pursuit of higher education through various academic and cultural workshops, as well as providing a sense of community through mentorship groups.
