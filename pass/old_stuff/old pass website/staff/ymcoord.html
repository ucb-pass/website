Internal Affairs

The Internal Affairs component serves as the 'back-bone' of our organization. It currently holds 5 different positions, all with the responsibility of keeping PASS running smoothly and efficiently.

Recruitment

The recruitment component of PASS seeks to promote awareness of access to higher education. PASS aims to spread knowledge and share experiences by encouraging our future leaders to pursue higher education.

Retention

PASS serves as a resource to the Pilipino campus population by providing academic resources and social services. Working closely with the Cesar Chavez Learning Center, we strive to build a community of academically, politically and socially retained individuals.

Tell Us About Your Position

As YM Coordinator, I closely work with the Galing Bata After-School Program & Staff and YM mentors. I recruit, train, coordinate, and overall, bond with mentors to establish a trusting and healthy working relationship, and encourage mentors to take ownership of the program. Every other week, I facilitate an Education and Ethnic Studies Field Studies Approved seminar that aims to explore issues concerning minorities in education, current education policies, and bilingual education.

Fall/Spring Programming:

1. Monthly Carnivals

2. Fall/Spring YM Retreat, Mentor Bonding Activities [MBAs], & Banquet

3. Spring Shadow Day(s): Mentees take a field trip to Cal.

My goal is to ultimately share the PASSION of YM with others. It is not just about going out to mentor at least once a week. We do what we do for the COMMUNITY. I want foster a tight-knit community with the GB staff, mentees, fellow mentors, and the YM team. In the end, I hope mentors realize the importance of mentorship and strive to become the ideal mentor as an Ate or Kuya (older sister or brother, respectively).

What part of PASS Hystory or Pilipin@ Hystory would you like to observe or experience?

I want to explore Pilipin@ Hystory through learning and doing more Traditional/Folk dances. As for PASS Hystory, I want to experience a SUCCESSFUL TRANSITION year with PASS 28 that archives everything--hystory that was lost in the past, rediscovered in the present, and will be made in the future.
