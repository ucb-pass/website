Internal Affairs

The Internal Affairs component serves as the 'back-bone' of our organization. It currently holds 5 different positions, all with the responsibility of keeping PASS running smoothly and efficiently.

Recruitment

The recruitment component of PASS seeks to promote awareness of access to higher education. PASS aims to spread knowledge and share experiences by encouraging our future leaders to pursue higher education.

Retention

PASS serves as a resource to the Pilipino campus population by providing academic resources and social services. Working closely with the Cesar Chavez Learning Center, we strive to build a community of academically, politically and socially retained individuals.

Tell Us About Your Position

Justine:

The major programming under the Outreach component includes Bay Area Outreach during the fall, Central California Outreach during winter break (the week prior to the beginning of the spring semester), and Southern California Outreach during spring break. Besides general outreach programming, we also provide feedback and advice for personal statements in the fall and appeals in the spring.

The Outreach component aims to reach out to underserved, underprivileged, and underresourced high schools and community colleges and provide them support in their path to higher education, with particular attention to Pilipin@ presence in higher education. We also want to empower students who are already in institutions of higher education (particularly within the Pilipin@ community at Cal) to inspire and help those who are still on their path to higher education. Generally, we want to increase the accessibility of higher education.
