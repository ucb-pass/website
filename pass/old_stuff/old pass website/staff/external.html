Internal Affairs

The Internal Affairs component serves as the 'back-bone' of our organization. It currently holds 5 different positions, all with the responsibility of keeping PASS running smoothly and efficiently.

Recruitment

The recruitment component of PASS seeks to promote awareness of access to higher education. PASS aims to spread knowledge and share experiences by encouraging our future leaders to pursue higher education.

Retention

PASS serves as a resource to the Pilipino campus population by providing academic resources and social services. Working closely with the Cesar Chavez Learning Center, we strive to build a community of academically, politically and socially retained individuals.

Tell Us About Your Position

As the External Relations Coordinator, I am responsible for maintaining a strong relationship with PASS Alumni and promoting the mission of PASS by extending the organization's networks. Many of these responsibilities also entail updating the PASS website, facebook page, and tumblr as well as publishing our bi-monthly newsletter "PASS IT ON". It is also my responsibility to help other staff members with their programming such as with Pathways and Friends of PASS. The greatest part of my position is engaging with other people and talking to them about ways to promote higher education. The work that PASS has done continuously improves student life and the quality of education that UC Berkeley offers.

What part of PASS Hystory or Pilipin@ Hystory would you like to observe or experience?

I want to see the life of Pilipin@s in the 20th century both in the United States and in the Philippines. I am especially interested in the era of the Great Depression and the 1960s-1990s.
