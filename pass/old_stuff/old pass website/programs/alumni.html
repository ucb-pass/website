External Relations Coordinator

PASS has been working diligently all semester to provide its services to students everywhere - both at Berkeley and the greater California. We could not be any more proud of the work we've been able to produce; undoubtedly, all of it would not have been possible if it weren’t for our awesome interns and the great support we receive from the Pilipino and bridges multicultural communities at Cal.

If you have any questions with how to get more involved with PASS, contact pass.ext.relations@gmail.com.
