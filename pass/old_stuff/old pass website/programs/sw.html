Senior Weekend

Senior Weekend was created in 1997 in response to the passage of Proposition 209 that eliminated race, ethnicity, and gender preferences in admissions, hiring and contracting for public institutions. Now that admissions of students of color has severely dropped, Senior Weekend has become an ever critical program for the spring semester. It is a 3 day and 3 night event that invites students all over the state to experience higher education and the Pilipin@ community here at Cal.The weekend includes academic and cultural workshops, critical dialogues, campus tours, student panels, a scavenger hunt in San Francisco, food and fun!
